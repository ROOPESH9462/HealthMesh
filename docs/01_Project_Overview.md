# Project Overview

## Introduction

The AI Healthcare Management Platform is a web application designed to digitize hospital operations while integrating Artificial Intelligence into healthcare workflows.

The platform allows patients to book appointments, doctors to manage patient records, hospitals to monitor analytics, and AI services to assist in diagnosis and report analysis.

---

## Objectives

- Reduce paperwork
- Improve healthcare efficiency
- Automate hospital workflows
- Assist doctors using AI
- Improve patient experience

---

## Target Users

- Hospitals
- Clinics
- Doctors
- Patients
- Pharmacists

---

## Major Modules

- Authentication
- Appointment Management
- Medical Records
- Billing
- Pharmacy
- AI Services
- Video Consultation
- Analytics Dashboard
